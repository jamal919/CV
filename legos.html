Md Jamal Uddin Khan is a Ph.D. student at Laboratoire d'Etudes en Géophysique et Océanographie Spatiales in France. His research interest includes numerical modeling in oceanography, climate change, and hydrology. Currently, he is studying the inundation mechanism during extreme events, like cyclone, in the Bay of Bengal.
